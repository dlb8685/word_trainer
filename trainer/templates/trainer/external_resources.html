Books

Most of the serious printed literature is aimed at the Scrabble® market. While the board is different, and the word lists are not 100% interchangeable, they are directionally similar.

  • Joe Edley, John Williams - Everything Scrabble: Third Edition
  • Joel Wapnick - How to Play SCRABBLE Like a Champion
  • Stefan Fatsis - Word Freak: Heartbreak, Triumph, Genius, and Obsession in the World of Competitive Scrabble Players
  • Mike Baron, Brian Cappelletto - SCRABBLE Wordbook
  • Robert Gillis - Bob's Bible: Words, Anagrams and Hooks
  • Saleem Ahmed - The Scrabble Word-Building Book: Updated Edition
