O'Laughlin Words

This page contains a list of words with high O'Laughlin playability. You can read more about that metric here. In short, it measures how important it is to know a certain word, given the likelihood of drawing the tiles to make that word, its point value, and the lack of other words to make with the same letters.

It is very important to know the words on this list and memorize them. Fortunately, The Word Trainer has a number of learning tools to facilitate this process.
