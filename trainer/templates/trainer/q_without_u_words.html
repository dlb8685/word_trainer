Q-Without-U Words

Below is a full list of words with a Q that do not have a U.

Pulling a high-value letter like Q can be a mixed blessing. On the one hand, it is a high scoring letter in many word games. On the other hand, the vast majority of Q words also include a U.

Towards the end of a game, every U might already have been played. If you are caught in this situation, it is absolutely essential to know which words can use a Q without a U.

Fortunately, The Word Trainer has a number of learning tools to facilitate this process.
