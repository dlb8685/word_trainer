One difficult position in many word games is that of finding one's self with a rack of nearly all vowels, or all consonants.

There are a couple of ways to handle this situation.

One is to simply forfeit the turn and exchange one's tiles.

However, by learning some words which are very heavy in vowels or consonants, there are many cases where a 5, 6, or even 7 letter word could be sitting on the board.

Fortunately, The Word Trainer has a number of learning tools to facilitate this process.
